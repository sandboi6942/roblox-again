Important Announcement: Roblox Service Closure Notice

Dear Roblox Community,

We hope this email finds you well. We are reaching out to you today with an important announcement regarding the future of Roblox. After careful consideration and evaluation of our platform's sustainability, we regret to inform you that Roblox will be shutting down in one month's time.

This decision has not been made lightly, and we understand the impact it will have on our dedicated community of players, developers, and fans. We want to express our deepest gratitude for your unwavering support over the years. Your creativity, passion, and enthusiasm have made Roblox the incredible platform it is today.

As we prepare to wind down operations, we want to ensure a smooth transition for all our users. Here are some key details about the shutdown process:

  • Shutdown Date: The official date for the shutdown of Roblox will be the 18th of March. On this day, access to the platform will be discontinued, and all services will be suspended.
  • Closure of Services: In the coming weeks leading up to the shutdown, certain features and services may be gradually disabled or limited. This includes in-game purchases, virtual item sales, and developer tools.
  • Refunds and Account Closure: We understand that many of you may have invested time and money into Roblox, and we want to ensure a fair process for refunds and account closure. Details on how to request refunds and close your account will be provided in a follow-up email.
  • Preservation of Content: While the platform will no longer be accessible for gameplay, we are exploring options to preserve and archive user-generated content where possible. More information on this will be shared as it becomes available.

We know that this news may come as a disappointment, and we sincerely apologize for any inconvenience it may cause. We are immensely grateful for the incredible community that has grown around Roblox, and we want to thank each and every one of you for your contributions.

As we reflect on the journey we've taken together, we're reminded of the countless memories created within the Roblox universe. From building towering structures to exploring virtual worlds, Roblox has been a place where imaginations have run wild and friendships have been forged.

We understand that Roblox holds a special place in the hearts of many, and the decision to shut down the platform was not made lightly. However, as technology evolves and new challenges arise, we must adapt to ensure the long-term sustainability of our services.

Throughout the years, we've witnessed the incredible growth and evolution of the Roblox community. From humble beginnings to a global phenomenon, Roblox has been a testament to the power of creativity and collaboration. We've been inspired by your passion and dedication, and we're grateful for the opportunity to be part of your digital adventures.

While the end of Roblox marks the close of a chapter, it also opens the door to new opportunities and experiences. As we bid farewell to the platform, we invite you to celebrate the memories made and the friendships formed. Let us cherish the moments we've shared and look forward to the adventures that lie ahead.

We want to express our deepest gratitude to each and every one of you for your support and enthusiasm over the years. Your contributions have shaped Roblox into the vibrant community it is today, and we are forever grateful for your involvement.

As we embark on this final month together, let us make the most of our time on Roblox. Let us come together to celebrate the creativity, friendship, and joy that have defined our shared experience. And let us carry these memories with us as we journey into the future.

In the meantime, we encourage you to cherish the remaining time on Roblox, make lasting memories with friends, and continue to unleash your creativity in the digital world.

If you have any questions or concerns about the shutdown process, please don't hesitate to reach out to our support team at support.roblox@mac.com.

Thank you for being part of the Roblox community. It has been an honor and a privilege to serve you.
